Hi, I'm Ryan

I am a software developer living in San Francisco seeking full-time employment at a growth minded, tech-focused firm. My passion for tech led me to Stanford, where I first learned to code while focusing my studies on technology, entrepreneurialism, and management science. After graduaitng in 2014 with a B.A. in Science, Technology, and Society (yep, that's a thing), I went to work at Oracle, where I sold cloud products to enterprise customers. In October of 2015, I left Oracle to join TeamCo Advisers, a SF based fund of hedge funds, where I worked as an investment analyst on a team of five responsible for allocating $1.7bn to hedge fund portfolios for institutional investors. While at TeamCo, I was involved in the automation of several processes and my passion for coding was invigorated. This revitalization, combined with industry headwinds in the fund of funds space, led me to persue software development as a full time career.
